Rules of the game

At the start of the game, the cards are randomly placed face down on the screen.

The player opens any two cards. If the color on them matches, then they remain open, and the player reveals the next two cards. If the cards do not match in color, then both cards are turned over and the player starts the turn again. Irok must open all the cards before the time runs out

The game ends when all the cards on the table are exposed, or if time has run out.
